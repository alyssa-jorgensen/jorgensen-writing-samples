About Me

I graduated from Virginia Tech with a Bachelor's degree in English and a certificate in professional and technical writing. I am currently pursuing my Masters of English.

I am passionate about public policy, environmental science and advocacy, and literature.

I can provides services in copyediting and proofreading across a wide variety of genres and topics. My ability to translate complex information into plain language makes me an ideal candidate for any science communication project. My creativity and narrative knowledge makes me a qualifiable candidate for any creative project.

I have previous experience working on a volunteer guide for Mountain Valley Watch, an organization dedicated to tracking the environmntal impact of the Mountain Valley Pipeline. I have also written a grant proposal and recommendation report for Adaire Theatre, a theatre company local to Pulaski, VA.

I have additional skills in HTML5 and video/audio editing.

Reach out to me at my email acjorgensen00@vt.edu if you wish to collaborate.
